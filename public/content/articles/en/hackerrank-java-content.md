I enjoy solving problems, and I particularly value exploring multiple approaches while doing so. With this motivation, I am starting a series of articles that cover solutions to all Java problems available on HackerRank. In this series, I will not only solve the problems but also discuss the data structures, design patterns, and algorithms used during the problem-solving process. My primary objective is not merely to present a final solution, but to explain how to approach a problem systematically and how a solution strategy is constructed step by step.

The series covers problems ranging from easy to intermediate difficulty. I have previously solved nearly all of these problems; however, through this series, I aim to diversify the solutions and document them in written form. For each problem, I make a deliberate effort to provide the following whenever possible:

- Brute force approaches,
- More efficient and optimized solutions,
- Evaluations of time and space complexity
